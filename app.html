About Our E-Waste Management System

The E-Waste Management System is a smart web-based platform designed to help individuals safely dispose of electronic waste. Our system connects users with authorized recycling centers and ensures environmentally responsible disposal of electronic products.

Our Mission

To create awareness about e-waste hazards and provide an easy, secure, and eco-friendly way to recycle electronic waste.

Our Vision

To build a cleaner and greener future by reducing electronic waste pollution and promoting sustainable recycling practices.

Why E-Waste Management is Important?

  • Prevents harmful toxins from polluting soil and water
  • Protects the environment
  • Promotes recycling and resource recovery
  • Encourages responsible citizen participation

Key Features of Our System

  • Secure User Registration & Login
  • Nearby Recycling Center Locator
  • Pickup Request Scheduling
  • Database Management of E-Waste Records
  • Tracking & Monitoring System

Environmental Impact

By using this platform, users contribute to reducing landfill waste, conserving natural resources, and minimizing environmental pollution. Together, we can make our planet safer and greener.
